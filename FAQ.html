Game Rules

Our game consists of 6 individual seasons for players. There will be 5 6 rounds of games and the final season will consist of 8 rounds so 7 seasons within the real world 38 game season. Players will pay entry of Â£10 at the start of each new season. The prize money will be split among the top two or three players depending on how many entries there are. Players will be presented with all the PL fixtures for that week and will have to predict the outcome of each either a home win/ draw or away win. A correct prediction will yield five points and an incorrect prediction will deduct 1 point from the current round total.

Each individual round will have an opportunity for bonus points over three different games:

  • Game 1: Correct score. If a player correctly predicts the correct score of said game they will be awarded 15 bonus points.
  • Game 2: Correct total match goals. If a player correctly predicts the exact amount of goals in said game they will receive 10 bonus points.
  • Game 3: Correct time of first goal. 7 options for this multiple choice: first goal in 0-15min, 15-30 mins,30-halftime. 45-60, 60-
